Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a 10-yard dumpster rental cost nationwide?

Prices range from $320 to $550, depending on location, debris type, and weight limits. Major cities like Dallas, Miami, and Seattle typically fall in the $375–$475 range.

How soon can I get a dumpster delivered in the United States?

In most metro areas (e.g., Phoenix, Charlotte, Denver), delivery is available within 24 hours. Rural areas may take 1–2 business days

What can’t I put in a roll-off dumpster?

Hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, tires, batteries, and asbestos are prohibited nationwide. Mattresses and appliances may incur fees.

Do I need a permit for a dumpster on the street?

Yes, if placing the bin on public right-of-way (e.g., street or sidewalk). Our local partners help you determine permit needs in your city.

How long can I keep a rented dumpster?

Standard rental periods are 7–10 days nationwide. Extensions are usually available for a small daily fee.

Ventnor City, NJ Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Ventnor City is a city situated on the Jersey Shore on Absecon Island, within Atlantic County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey, on the Atlantic Ocean. As of the 2020 United States census, the city's population was 9,210, a decrease of 1,440 (−13.5%) from the 2010 census count of 10,650, which in turn reflected a decrease of 2,260 (−17.5%) from the 12,910 counted in the 2000 census. The city, and all of Atlantic County, is part of the Atlantic City-Hammonton metropolitan statistical area, which is part of the Philadelphia-Wilmington-Camden, PA-NJ-DE-MD combined statistical area, also known as the Delaware Valley or Philadelphia metropolitan area.
